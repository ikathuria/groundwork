# Silver Supply and Cost Scaling Risk

## What It Is

Silver supply and cost scaling risk is the commercial reason to investigate copper substitution. It is not a device failure, but it can make a technically excellent silver design economically or materially unattractive at terawatt PV scale.

## How It Manifests

- High metallization paste cost.
- Exposure to silver price volatility.
- High absolute silver demand as PV manufacturing scales.
- Roadmap pressure to reduce silver per watt.

## How To Detect It

- Track silver mg/cell and mg/W.
- Compare material cost per watt for silver versus copper routes.
- Use ITRPV or equivalent roadmaps for market-share and material-intensity assumptions.

## Mitigation

- Reduce silver paste laydown with improved screen/print processes.
- Replace some silver with copper paste or plated copper.
- Use silver-coated copper only if it preserves reliability and solderability.
