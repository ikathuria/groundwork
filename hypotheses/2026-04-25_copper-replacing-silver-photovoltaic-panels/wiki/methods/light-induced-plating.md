# Light-Induced Plating

## What It Measures / Does

Light-induced plating uses illumination and an external circuit condition to plate metal contacts on an operating solar-cell structure. It is often discussed for copper metallization because the illuminated cell can drive current in contact regions during plating.

## When To Use It

Use when the copper contact route needs high-aspect-ratio plated fingers with lower silver use and the lab can control illumination, plating bath, current conditions, and patterned openings.

## Key Parameters

- Illumination intensity and current density.
- Plating bath composition and agitation.
- Pattern quality and exposed contact area.
- Capping layer selection after copper deposition.
