# Photovoltaic Silver-Use Roadmapping

## What It Measures / Does

Silver-use roadmapping estimates metallization material demand and technology share as PV manufacturing scales. It is not an experiment method by itself, but it defines why copper replacement is worth testing and what success would mean commercially.

## When To Use It

Use when deciding whether a lab-scale copper result matters for manufacturing. It helps translate "same efficiency" into material demand, cost, and supply-chain relevance.

## Key Parameters

- Silver per cell and silver per watt.
- Market share of PERC, TOPCon, and SHJ technologies.
- Share of copper-containing metallization and interconnection methods.
- Module and cell efficiency assumptions.
