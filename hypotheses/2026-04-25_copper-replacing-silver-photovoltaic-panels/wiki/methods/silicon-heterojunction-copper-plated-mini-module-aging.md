# Silicon Heterojunction Copper-Plated Mini-Module Aging

## What It Measures / Does

This method packages copper-metallized SHJ cells into mini-modules and subjects them to aging tests. It captures risks that bare-cell tests miss: encapsulant chemistry, interconnection stress, and module-level electrical loss.

## When To Use It

Use after a copper SHJ contact stack has promising cell-level results. It is the right bridge between contact fabrication and a credible module reliability claim.

## Key Parameters

- SHJ cell stack and metallization route.
- Encapsulant and interconnect material.
- Damp heat / thermal cycling stress sequence.
- Mini-module power retention and failure localization.
