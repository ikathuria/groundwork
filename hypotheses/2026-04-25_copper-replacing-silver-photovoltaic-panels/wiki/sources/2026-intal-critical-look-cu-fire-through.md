# Critical look at atmospheric Cu fire-through dielectric metallization

## Summary

Intal et al. study atmospheric copper fire-through dielectric metallization for p-PERC solar cells, using laser-enhanced contact optimization to improve the Cu-Si interface. This source is a recent positive but still preliminary signal for more silver-free contact strategies.

## Key Claims

- Stable copper silicide interface formation is treated as essential for cost-effective copper contacts.
- Copper diffusivity, electromigration, and hillock formation are called out as reliability challenges.
- Laser-enhanced contact optimization reduces series resistance and improves fill factor in the reported PERC samples.
- The paper frames LECO-treated copper contacts as a scalable route toward reliable silver-free metallization.
