# DuPont Solamet Photovoltaic Metallizations Brochure

## Summary

This supplier brochure documents commercial photovoltaic metallization paste families and serves as the silver-paste baseline for material selection. It does not test copper replacement directly, but it anchors what the control arm of the experimental plan would be replacing.

## Key Claims

- Solamet products are commercial photovoltaic metallization pastes used in silicon solar-cell production.
- A copper-substitution plan should compare against a current-generation silver paste baseline rather than an unspecified "silver contact."
- Supplier material selection affects firing, contact resistance, solderability, and process compatibility.
