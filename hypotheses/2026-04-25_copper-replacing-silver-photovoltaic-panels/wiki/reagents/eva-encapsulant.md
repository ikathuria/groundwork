# EVA Encapsulant

## Properties

EVA is a common photovoltaic module encapsulant. In copper metallization work, it matters because damp-heat exposure and encapsulant chemistry can interact with contact corrosion and copper migration.

## Common Uses

- Commercial crystalline silicon modules.
- Encapsulation in damp-heat and temperature-cycling tests.

## Alternatives

- [[tpo-encapsulant]]
- Other polyolefin elastomer encapsulants.

## Gotchas

- Moisture ingress and encapsulant degradation can increase series resistance and electrode corrosion.
- Karas et al. discuss encapsulant chemistry as a possible driving force for copper outdiffusion.
