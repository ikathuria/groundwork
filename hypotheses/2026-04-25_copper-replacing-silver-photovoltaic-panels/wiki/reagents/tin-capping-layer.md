# Tin Capping Layer

## Properties

Tin capping layers are used over copper-plated contacts to protect copper and support interconnection. Karas et al. show that tin caps do not automatically stop copper mobility under damp heat.

## Common Uses

- Ni/Cu/Sn plated stacks.
- Solderable capping over copper conductors.

## Alternatives

- Silver capping.
- Engineered multilayer capping stacks.

## Gotchas

- [[../failure-modes/copper-outdiffusion-during-damp-heat]]
- Intermetallic formation and voids may evolve during damp heat.
