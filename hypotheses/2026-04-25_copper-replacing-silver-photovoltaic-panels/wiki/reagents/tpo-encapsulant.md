# TPO / Polyolefin Encapsulant

## Properties

Polyolefin encapsulants are alternatives to EVA for PV module packaging. They are relevant for copper metallization because packaging chemistry can affect corrosion and copper mobility under damp heat.

## Common Uses

- Mini-module aging tests.
- Reliability experiments comparing encapsulant-driven degradation.

## Alternatives

- [[eva-encapsulant]]

## Gotchas

- Encapsulant choice can change measured copper reliability; do not generalize across packages without testing.
