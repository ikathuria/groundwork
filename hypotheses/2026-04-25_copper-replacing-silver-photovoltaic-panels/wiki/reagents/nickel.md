# Nickel

## Properties

Nickel is used as a seed layer, adhesion layer, silicide-forming layer, and diffusion barrier between copper and silicon. It is central to plated copper contact stacks.

## Common Uses

- [[../methods/nickel-diffusion-barrier-plating]]
- Ni/Cu/Sn and Ni/Cu/Ag contact stacks.
- Nickel silicide formation before copper deposition.

## Alternatives

- Nickel-cobalt barriers.
- Other transition metal barriers, depending on process compatibility.

## Gotchas

- Barrier continuity and thickness control matter.
- Nickel can add contact-resistance concerns if the stack is not optimized.
