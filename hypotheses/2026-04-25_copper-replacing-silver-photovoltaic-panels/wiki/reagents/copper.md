# Copper

## Properties

Copper is the proposed lower-cost conductor for replacing silver-intensive photovoltaic metallization. It has high conductivity and much lower commodity cost than silver, but its mobility in silicon and packaging stacks makes barrier design essential.

## Common Uses

- Main conductor in [[../methods/nickel-copper-plated-silicon-metallization]].
- Conductive phase in [[copper-metallization-paste]].
- Copper wires for module interconnection in roadmap context.

## Alternatives

- [[silver]]
- [[silver-conductive-paste]]
- Silver-coated copper paste particles.
