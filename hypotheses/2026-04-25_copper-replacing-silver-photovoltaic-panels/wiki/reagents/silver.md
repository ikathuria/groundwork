# Silver

## Properties

Silver is the incumbent high-conductivity metal used in crystalline silicon solar-cell metallization, usually as a paste. It is technically effective but cost and material-scale pressure motivate copper substitution.

## Common Uses

- Front-side and rear-side metallization paste in PERC, TOPCon, and SHJ cells.
- Silver capping layers in some plated copper contact stacks.
- Narrow fire-through contact patterns under copper paste in some hybrid designs.

## Alternatives

- [[copper]]
- [[copper-metallization-paste]]
- Silver-coated copper particles.
