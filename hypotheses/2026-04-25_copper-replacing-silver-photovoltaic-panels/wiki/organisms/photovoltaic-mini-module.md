# Photovoltaic Mini-Module

## Growth / Operating Conditions

Mini-modules package cells with encapsulants, glass/backsheet or equivalent layers, and interconnects. They are the smallest practical system for testing whether copper metallization remains stable in a module-like environment.

## Common Pitfalls

- Bare-cell stability can fail after encapsulation.
- Encapsulant chemistry can change copper outdiffusion behavior.
- Thermal cycling can stress interconnects and metal grids.
