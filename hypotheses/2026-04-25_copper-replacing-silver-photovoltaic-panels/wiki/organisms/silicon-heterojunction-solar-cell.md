# Silicon Heterojunction Solar Cell

## Growth / Operating Conditions

SHJ cells combine crystalline silicon with thin passivating and contact layers. Their low-temperature process constraints make metallization choice especially important.

## Common Pitfalls

- Contact materials must preserve passivation and transparent conductive oxide performance.
- Copper-containing routes need reliability validation under damp heat and thermal cycling.
- Silver use can be high in SHJ, increasing motivation for copper-containing alternatives.
