# TOPCon Solar Cell

## Growth / Operating Conditions

TOPCon cells are included as a roadmap and silver-use context system. The accepted raw corpus did not fetch a complete TOPCon copper-plating process paper, so Pass 3 should not choose TOPCon as the primary experimental platform unless new sources are ingested.

## Common Pitfalls

- Higher n-type silver use is a major motivation, but it is not proof that copper is process-ready.
- The Pass 1 corpus has a TOPCon source gap due to blocked publisher/proceedings fetches.
