# IBC Solar Cell

## Growth / Operating Conditions

IBC cells are back-contact crystalline silicon devices. In this corpus, Chen et al. use IBC cells to replace most silver with copper paste on rear fingers and busbars while retaining narrow silver contact regions.

## Common Pitfalls

- Copper paste line resistance can be higher than silver paste line resistance.
- The retained silver contact/barrier pattern means the route is low-silver, not fully silver-free.
- Reliability depends on the rear contact stack and damp-heat / thermal-stress exposure.
