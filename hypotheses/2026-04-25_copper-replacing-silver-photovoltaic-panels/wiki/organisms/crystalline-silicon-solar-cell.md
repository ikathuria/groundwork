# Crystalline Silicon Solar Cell

## Growth / Operating Conditions

Crystalline silicon solar cells are the baseline device system for this hypothesis. They are not living organisms, but they occupy the "organisms" slot in the GROUNDWORK schema because they are the experimental system being manipulated.

## Common Pitfalls

- Copper can introduce recombination-active contamination if it reaches silicon.
- Silver reduction strategies can change contact resistance and fill factor.
- Reliability claims require encapsulated-cell or module-level tests, not only bare-cell I-V data.
