# PERC Solar Cell

## Growth / Operating Conditions

PERC cells are a commercial crystalline silicon architecture and a practical testbed for screen-printing and fire-through metallization experiments.

## Common Pitfalls

- Copper fire-through contacts must avoid degrading emitter/passivation quality.
- Shared furnace use can create copper-contamination concerns for silver control cells.
- Series resistance and fill-factor changes need direct measurement after processing.
