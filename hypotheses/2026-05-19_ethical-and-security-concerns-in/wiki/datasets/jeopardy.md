The Jeopardy! question generation dataset contains Jeopardy! clue-answer pairs used for evaluating knowledge-grounded generation (generate a Jeopardy! clue given an answer entity). Used in the original RAG paper ([[sources/2020-lewis-rag]]) to demonstrate RAG's advantage in knowledge-grounded generation beyond QA.
