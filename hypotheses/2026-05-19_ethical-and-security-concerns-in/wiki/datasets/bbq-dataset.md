BBQ (Bias Benchmark for QA) is a dataset of question templates designed to evaluate social bias across nine categories including age, race, gender, religion, and disability. Used in RAG fairness research to measure whether RAG systems produce more or less biased outputs compared to standalone LLMs, particularly for underrepresented demographic groups.
