WinoBias is a benchmark for evaluating gender bias in coreference resolution and language model outputs. It contains sentences about occupations paired with gendered pronouns, testing whether models exhibit occupational gender stereotypes. Used in RAG fairness evaluation to measure whether RAG amplifies gender biases present in retrieved corpora.
