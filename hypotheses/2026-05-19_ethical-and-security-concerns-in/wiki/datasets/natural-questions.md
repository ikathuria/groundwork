Natural Questions (Google) is an original open-domain QA benchmark from real Google Search queries with Wikipedia-sourced answers. Used as a primary evaluation benchmark in the original RAG paper ([[sources/2020-lewis-rag]]). Largely superseded by the NQ-Open variant ([[datasets/nq-dataset]]) in subsequent work.
