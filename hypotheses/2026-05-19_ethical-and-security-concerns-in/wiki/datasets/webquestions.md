WebQuestions is a QA dataset built from Google Suggest queries answered using Freebase. Used in the original RAG paper ([[sources/2020-lewis-rag]]) for open-domain QA evaluation. Now largely deprecated in favor of NQ-based benchmarks due to Freebase's discontinuation.
