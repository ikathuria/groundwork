ASQA (Answer Summaries for Questions which are Ambiguous) is a long-form QA dataset requiring responses with citations to multiple Wikipedia passages. Tests citation accuracy and factual grounding — directly relevant to faithfulness in RAG systems. Used in Self-RAG ([[sources/2023-asai-self-rag]]) to evaluate citation precision and recall.
