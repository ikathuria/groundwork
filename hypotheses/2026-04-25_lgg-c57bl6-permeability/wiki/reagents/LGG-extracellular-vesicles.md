# LGG-derived extracellular vesicles (EVs)

Membrane-bounded particles (~50–200 nm) shed by LGG into the surrounding medium. Wang et al. 2025 (PMC12360853) characterise LGG-EVs containing **MDP (muramyl dipeptide)** that activate NOD2 and signal anti-viral / anti-inflammatory pathways. Yang et al. 2019 (PMC6589071, in failed-403) reported EVs as the carrier vehicle for p40 release.

EVs are thus a **third candidate "active principle" of postbiotic LGG** alongside p40 and EPS, and their cargo (proteins + nucleic acids + glycolipids) gives them broader pharmacological surface area than either single molecule.
