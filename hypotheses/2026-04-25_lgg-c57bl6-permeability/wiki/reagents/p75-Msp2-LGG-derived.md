# p75 / Msp2 — LGG-derived secreted soluble protein

A second LGG-secreted soluble protein (~75 kDa) with epithelial cytoprotective activity, characterised alongside p40 by the Yan/Polk lab. Acts via similar EGFR-PI3K/Akt signalling but with a different target spectrum.

Less thoroughly characterised than p40, and largely supplanted by p40 as the headline LGG soluble-mediator in the post-2010 literature. Bäuerl 2010 (PMC3019367 — also in our failed-403 set) showed the orthologous p40/p75 from *L. casei* BL23 have similar barrier-protective activity, suggesting the mechanism is genus-conserved rather than LGG-strain-specific.
