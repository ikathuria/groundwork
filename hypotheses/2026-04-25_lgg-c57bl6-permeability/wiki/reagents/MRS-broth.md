# de Man, Rogosa & Sharpe (MRS) broth

The standard liquid growth medium for *Lactobacillus*. Selective via low pH (~6.5 reducing during growth), ammonium / Tween 80 / acetate buffering, and high peptone content. Solid form (MRS agar) used for [[../methods/bacterial-CFU-enumeration]].

## LGG-specific protocol

| Step | Parameters |
|---|---|
| Inoculation | from glycerol stock or MRS agar single colony |
| Volume | 10 mL starter → 100 mL working culture |
| Atmosphere | static, atmospheric (LGG is microaerotolerant); anaerobic for plate counts |
| Temperature | 37 °C |
| Time | 16 h to stationary; harvest at OD₆₀₀ ≈ 1.0 (mid-log) for dosing |
| Harvest | centrifuge 4 000 ×g 10 min, wash 2× with sterile PBS |
| Resuspend | in PBS or 10 % skim-milk PBS at desired CFU/mL |

## Pitfalls

- **Strain drift on long passage**: see [[../failure-modes/LGG-strain-drift-passage]]. Refresh from a sealed master glycerol stock every 6 months; never propagate through > 5 passages between cryostock and dosing.
- **Anaerobic vs aerobic CFU counts diverge**: LGG plates 1–2× higher anaerobically; pick one method and stay consistent.
