# Bacterial CFU enumeration

Viable-cell counting by serial dilution and plating on selective agar. The **dosing-QC backbone** of any probiotic study — without it, you do not actually know what dose the animals received.

## Two non-negotiable measurements

1. **Dose-day CFU** — plate the gavage suspension at the moment of dosing. Anything below 80 % of the design dose (1×10⁹ CFU/mouse) means the day is uninterpretable.
2. **Engraftment CFU** — plate fresh faeces from a subset of LGG mice and a subset of controls weekly. Confirms LGG colonisation in the treatment arm and rules out cross-contamination of controls (pairs with [[16S-rRNA-microbiome-sequencing]]).

## LGG-specific protocol

- **Selective medium**: MRS agar at pH 5.5 with 0.05 % L-cysteine (suppresses common contaminants while supporting Lactobacillus); incubate **anaerobically at 37 °C for 48 h**.
- **Differentiation from endogenous Lactobacillus**: MRS alone won't tell LGG from native *L. murinus* or *L. johnsonii*. Confirm strain identity by colony PCR (LGG-specific primers, e.g. *welE*) on a sample of colonies, or by 16S sequencing.
