# ELISA cytokine and barrier-marker quantification

Plate-based immunoassay for plasma / mucosal cytokines and barrier-translocation markers. In our context, the relevant analytes are:

- **TNF-α, IL-6, IL-1β** — pro-inflammatory; rise with barrier loss
- **IL-10** — anti-inflammatory; LGG mechanism (Si 2025: STING-dependent IL-10)
- **LBP (LPS-binding protein)** — systemic translocation surrogate; preferred over plasma LPS itself for chronic studies
- **Lipocalin-2 / faecal calprotectin** — luminal inflammation markers

## Recommended platform

For a small mouse study (n ≤ 16/arm), single-analyte ELISAs are fine. For mechanism dissection across many cytokines, a **multiplex Luminex or MSD panel** is more efficient and uses ~25 µL plasma/sample (relevant when mouse blood volumes are limiting).
