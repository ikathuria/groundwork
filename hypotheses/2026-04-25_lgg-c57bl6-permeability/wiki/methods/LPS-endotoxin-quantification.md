# Plasma LPS / endotoxin quantification

Two complementary platforms:

- **LAL (Limulus amoebocyte lysate)** — direct LPS measurement; gold standard but exquisitely sensitive to environmental contamination (single fingerprint = false positive).
- **LBP ELISA** — plasma LPS-binding protein as an integrated proxy for translocation over hours-days. More robust to handling, less time-resolved.

## Why pair with FITC-dextran

FD-4 reports small-molecule paracellular flux at one timepoint. LBP reports systemic gram-negative-derived translocation integrated over recent days. If LGG suppresses FD-4 but LBP is unchanged, the barrier improvement is restricted to the small-molecule pathway. If both move together, the effect is broader.
