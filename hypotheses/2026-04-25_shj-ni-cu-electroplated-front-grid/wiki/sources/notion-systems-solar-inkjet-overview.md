# Notion Systems — Solar Inkjet Equipment Overview

**Notion Systems GmbH** — *2024 (current product page)*. URL: https://www.notion-systems.com/products/Industrial-solutions/solar.html

## Summary

Vendor product-overview page for Notion Systems' industrial inkjet platforms targeted at c-Si and thin-film solar manufacturing. Notion Systems is one of the two primary industrial inkjet equipment suppliers (alongside SUSS PiXDRO) for plating-resist patterning in plated Ni/Cu metallization workflows for SHJ and TOPCon. Catalog entry establishing supplier availability for the [[methods/inkjet-mask-patterning]] step in the experimental plan.

## Key claims / specifications

- **Vendor positioning:** "Inkjet-printed solar cells offer several advantages over traditional manufacturing techniques. The precise control of ink droplets allows for the optimization of material usage and reduces waste."
- **Crystalline-Si solar inkjet (relevant arm):** "introduction of new or improved cell-concepts, additive technologies and in particular, inkjet plays a major role". Notion's c-Si inkjet platforms target plating-mask deposition, hot-melt resist, and other additive patterning steps in the cell line.
- **Thin-film solar inkjet (secondary arm):** structured functional-layer deposition for cost-per-Wp reduction; not directly relevant to SHJ Ni/Cu but illustrates the breadth of the vendor's offering.
- **Equipment options listed:**
  - Automation from box or carrier
  - Individual process unit design
  - Manual or fully automated printhead cleaning
  - Process stage for different substrate sizes (M6/M10/G12 wafer compatibility implied)
- **Industrial solutions footprint:** Display, Electronics, Semiconductor, Glass, 3D — confirms a deep inkjet-equipment expertise base feeding into the solar platform.
- **Specific PV-step the equipment serves (inferred from industry literature):** plating-resist mask deposition prior to electroplating, replacing photolithography for selective Ni/Cu growth.
