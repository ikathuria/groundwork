# Solar — Notion Systems GmbH (industrial inkjet for c-Si and thin-film)

## Summary
Notion Systems GmbH (Schwetzingen, Germany) markets industrial inkjet printing equipment for both crystalline-Si solar (cSi) and thin-film solar applications. The product page is a high-level industry overview rather than a TDS — it describes Notion's role as a vendor of inkjet platforms (n.jet evo, n.jet EHD) capable of structured deposition of functional layers. For this hypothesis, Notion Systems is the reference supplier for inkjet-printed plating-resist masks: an alternative low-cost patterning route to photolithography for selective Cu plating onto SHJ TCO. Hermans et al. (2014, cited in [[2018-pvtech-metallization-shj-cells-modules]] ref [21]) demonstrated SHJ-compatible inkjet hot-melt resist masking — Notion Systems is one of two vendors (with PiXDRO/Meyer Burger) that supplies the production-scale inkjet hardware to do this in-line.

## Key product specs
- Equipment lines:
  - **n.jet evo** — laboratory inkjet platform.
  - **n.jet EHD** — electrohydrodynamic inkjet variant (high-resolution, sub-droplet patterning).
  - **n.amr / n.amc** — semi-/fully-automated photolithography & wet processing tools up to 150 mm.
  - **n.unixx / n.varixx / n.chemixx / n.maxx** — semi-automatic and automatic systems for 200–300 mm or larger substrates.
- Industrial markets supported: Display, Electronics, Semiconductor, **Solar**, Glass, 3D printing.
- Solar offering — two segments:
  - **Crystalline Solar (cSi):** "Inkjet printing is the perfect technology to apply structured functional layers at low cost with optimal control of local and global layer properties. Additive technologies and in particular, inkjet plays a major role" in next-gen cell concepts.
  - **Thin Film Solar:** "as cSi solar, thin-film technology is driven by the need to introduce new processes increasing module output at ever lower cost per Watt peak."
- Options (Solar tools):
  - Automation from box or carrier
  - Individual process unit design
  - Manual or fully automated printhead cleaning
  - Process stage for different substrate sizes
  - Integration of multiple printheads
  - Infeed & outfeed module
  - Inline cleaning station
- Demo / R&D centers: Singen (Germany, R&D and Application Center) and Schwetzingen (Notion Advanced Application Center).
- Headquarters: Werkstr. 2, 68723 Schwetzingen, Germany. Phone +49 6202 57877-0; sales@notion-systems.com.
- Pricing: not listed (capital equipment — quote-based).
