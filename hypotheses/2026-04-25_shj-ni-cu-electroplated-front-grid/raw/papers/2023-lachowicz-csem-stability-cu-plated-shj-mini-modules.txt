SUMMARY AND OUTLOOK


▪ Module stability with Smartwire interconnection:

     ▪ PTC: very good stability with copper plated metallization ✓
     ▪ PID: very good stability with copper plated metallization ✓
     ▪ DH:  -5% after 3xIEC, better stability to prove
       (with 1-cell and medium size modules)


▪ In preparation: interconnection with soldered wires
▪ Ongoing cell experiments: thin ITO and dielectric layer = plating mask
  For In reduction
