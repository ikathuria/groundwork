# Summary

Crossref retraction notice (Elsevier / *Life Sciences* 257, retracted 2020-09-15) for "Effectiveness of mesenchymal stem cell-seeded onto the 3D polylactic acid/polycaprolactone/hydroxyapatite scaffold on the radius bone defect in rat" by Oryan et al. The article was published 2020-09-01 and retracted **two weeks later** (publisher-issued, no Retraction Watch record-id in this notice). Crossref notice **does not include a narrative reason**. A 2026 erratum (`10.1016/j.lfs.2025.124146`) is also linked.

# Original claim

- MSCs seeded onto a 3D-printed PLA/PCL/[[../reagents/hydroxyapatite]] composite scaffold accelerate bone repair in a rat radial-bone-defect model, evaluated radiologically, histologically, and biomechanically.
- A central reference (Hassanajili 2019) is the same group's earlier indirect-3D-printing PLA/PCL/HA scaffold characterization.

# Retraction status

- Retraction date: 2020-09-15 (Elsevier publisher-issued).
- Source: publisher only (no Retraction Watch record-id present in this notice).
- Reason: reason not stated in Crossref notice. Two-week post-publication retraction strongly suggests editorial intervention (possibly duplicate publication, image issue, or authorship/data-integrity concern), but not stated.
- Erratum follow-up: `10.1016/j.lfs.2025.124146` (2026).
- Author A. Oryan has multiple papers in this notice's reference list, raising self-citation pattern concerns.
