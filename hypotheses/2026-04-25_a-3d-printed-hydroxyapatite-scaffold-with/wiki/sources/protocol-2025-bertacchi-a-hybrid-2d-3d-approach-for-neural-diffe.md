# Summary

Bio-protocol for generating human telencephalic brain organoids from hiPSCs by a hybrid 2D/3D approach: 2D neural induction with small-molecule TGFβ/BMP/WNT inhibitors → dissociation → reaggregation in Matrigel droplets cultured in spinning mini-bioreactors (SpinΩ) or on orbital shakers, with optional FGF8 morphogen modulation for regional patterning.

**Out-of-domain for this hypothesis** (bone tissue engineering with HA scaffolds). Captured here only because the **hybrid 2D-induce → 3D-reaggregate** scaffolding methodology is conceptually adjacent to scaffold cell-seeding strategies (e.g., expand cells in 2D monolayer first, then seed onto a 3D porous HA scaffold). No reagents, organisms, or readouts in this protocol transfer directly to the HA-scaffold proliferation hypothesis. Page kept minimal.

# Key steps (condensed)

1. 2D neural induction of hiPSCs (HMGU1 line) with TGFβ/BMP/WNT inhibitors → neural progenitors in ~10 days.
2. Dissociate NPs; reaggregate in Matrigel droplets.
3. Culture in spinning mini-bioreactors (SpinΩ) or orbital shakers ~1 month → telencephalic organoids.
4. Optionally add FGF8 to medium for dose/time-dependent A/P–D/V regional patterning.
