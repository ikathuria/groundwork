# Summary

Crossref retraction notice (Royal Society of Chemistry / *Biomaterials Science* 11(9): 3365, published 2023-04-04) for the original 2018 article "Enhanced bone defect repairing effects in glucocorticoid-induced osteonecrosis of the femoral head using a porous nano-lithium-hydroxyapatite/gelatin microsphere/erythropoietin composite scaffold" (*Biomater. Sci.* 6: 519–537, DOI `10.1039/c7bm00975e`). Retraction reason **not stated in the Crossref notice**; the embedded JATS abstract is only a citation pointer.

# Original claim

- A porous nano-lithium-doped-HA / gelatin-microsphere / [[../reagents/erythropoietin|EPO]] composite scaffold enhances bone-defect repair in glucocorticoid-induced femoral-head osteonecrosis (rat or rabbit model).
