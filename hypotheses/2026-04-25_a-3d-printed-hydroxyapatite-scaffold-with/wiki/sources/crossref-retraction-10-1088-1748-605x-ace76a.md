# Summary

Crossref retraction notice (IOP Publishing / *Biomedical Materials* 18(5): 059701, published 2023-07-26) for the original 2021 article "Preparation and biological properties of silk fibroin / nano-hydroxyapatite / hyaluronic acid composite scaffold" (*Biomed. Mater.* 16: 045045, DOI `10.1088/1748-605x/ac08aa`). The Crossref notice contains **no author list and no narrative reason**.

# Original claim

- A silk-fibroin / nano-HA / hyaluronic-acid composite scaffold has favorable physico-chemical and biological properties (presumably mechanical, degradation, biocompatibility / osteogenic) suitable for bone tissue engineering.

# Retraction status

- Retraction date: 2023-07-26 (IOP).
- Sources: Retraction Watch record-id 46309, plus publisher-issued retraction.
- Reason: reason not stated in Crossref notice. Author list omitted from retraction notice.
- Original article was funded/authored from China (typical for the SF/HA/HAc cluster, though authors not listed in this notice).
