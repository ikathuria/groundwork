# Summary

Crossref retraction notice (Wiley / *Clinical Oral Implants Research* 29(7), retracted 2018-07-18; original online 2015-08-07) for an HA/β-TCP/collagen scaffold in vivo histomorphometry / radiology / SEM study at 15, 30, 60 days in an animal model. **Retraction reason explicit: image manipulation across publications and time points.**

# Original claim

- An HA/β-TCP/collagen composite scaffold supports in vivo bone formation in an animal model, characterized by histology, histomorphometry, radiology, and SEM at 15/30/60 days.

# Retraction status

- Retracted by agreement between the editor-in-chief (L. Heitz-Mayfield) and Wiley.
- Quoted reason: "The retraction has been agreed due to image discrepancies resulting in unreliable data. It has not been possible to prove the validity of the images. Images in Figure 4 have been used in other publications representing different time points and materials. Images within Figure 4 have been manipulated to represent different time points and materials."
