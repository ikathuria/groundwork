# Ammonium bicarbonate (NH₄HCO₃)

Volatile salt used as a sacrificial porogen in scaffold fabrication: decomposes at modest temperatures into NH₃ + CO₂ + H₂O, leaving voids. Mentioned in this wiki via a retracted eggshell-CaCO₃ scaffold paper.

## Properties

- **CAS:** 1066-33-7
- **Decomposition:** ~60 °C (vapour pressure significant at room T).

## Common uses in this hypothesis space

- Sacrificial pore-former in bulk scaffold fabrication.

## Gotchas / things to watch for

- [[../failure-modes/cytotoxic-residue-from-binders-or-photoinitiators]] — residual NH₃ is cytotoxic; thorough washing required.
