# Bone morphogenetic protein 7 (BMP-7 / OP-1)

Closely related to BMP-2; was clinically approved as OP-1 Implant for tibial non-unions but withdrawn in some markets. Less commonly used than BMP-2 in modern scaffold studies.

## Properties

- Same pathway as [[../reagents/BMP-2]]; SMAD1/5/8 → RUNX2.
- Slightly different receptor affinity profile.

## Common uses in this hypothesis space

- Comparator / alternative to BMP-2 in osteogenic induction.
