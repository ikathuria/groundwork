# Doxorubicin (DOX)

Anthracycline anticancer drug. In Wang 2024 polydopamine-CDHA scaffolds, DOX is loaded for local release at bone-tumour resection sites — bone repair scaffold + chemotherapy delivery.

## Properties

- **CAS:** 23214-92-8
- **MW:** 543.52 g/mol
- **Mechanism:** topoisomerase-II inhibitor; DNA-intercalator.

## Common uses in this hypothesis space

- Drug delivery from CDHA scaffolds for bone-cancer treatment.

## Gotchas / things to watch for

- Cytotoxic; handle and dispose per chemotherapy guidelines.
- Light-sensitive; aliquot and store dark.
