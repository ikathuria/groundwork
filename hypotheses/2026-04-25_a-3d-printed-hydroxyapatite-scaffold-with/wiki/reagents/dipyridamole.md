# Dipyridamole

Adenosine A2A receptor agonist (indirect — phosphodiesterase inhibitor). Used off-label as a pro-osteogenic small molecule in bone-defect studies; locally delivered from HA scaffolds in the Ishack 2015 mouse calvarial defect work.

## Properties

- **CAS:** 58-32-2
- **MW:** 504.63 g/mol

## Common uses in this hypothesis space

- Loaded onto 3D-printed HA scaffolds for local pro-osteogenic delivery in critical-size defects.

## Alternatives

- BMP-2, dex — more conventional osteogenic stimuli.

## Gotchas / things to watch for

- Off-label use; clinical translation requires regulatory novel-use approval.
