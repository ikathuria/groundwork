# Alcian Blue

Cationic phthalocyanine dye that stains sulphated and carboxylated GAGs (chondroitin sulphate, hyaluronate). Used here for the chondrogenic side of osteochondral / interface gradient cultures.

## Properties

- **CAS:** 33864-99-2
- **Working solution:** 1% in 3% acetic acid pH 2.5 (sulphated GAGs only) or pH 1.0.

## Common uses in this hypothesis space

- Chondrogenic side of zonal / gradient scaffolds.
