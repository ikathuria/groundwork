# Penicillin-streptomycin (P/S)

Standard antibiotic supplement for cell culture; 1× working concentration is 100 U/mL penicillin + 100 µg/mL streptomycin.

## Properties

- 100× stock; dilute 1:100 into media.

## Common uses in this hypothesis space

- 1% (v/v) supplement to basal media in essentially all cell-culture protocols here.

## Suppliers / catalog

| Supplier | Catalog | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Gibco | 15140-122 | most common |
| Sigma | P4333 | research |

## Alternatives

- Antibiotic-antimycotic (adds amphotericin B for fungal coverage).
- Gentamicin alone for streptomycin-allergic users.
- **Antibiotic-free culture** for long-term studies — masks contamination but is more rigorous.

## Gotchas / things to watch for

- Antibiotic use can mask low-level mycoplasma or fungal contamination — periodic mycoplasma testing is recommended regardless.
- Some studies suggest aminoglycosides affect MSC differentiation at sub-cytotoxic doses.
