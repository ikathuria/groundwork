# L-glutamine

Essential cell-culture amino-acid supplement (typically 2 mM final). Spontaneously degrades in solution to ammonia and pyroglutamate (~50% in 4 weeks at 37 °C); GlutaMAX (alanyl-glutamine dipeptide) is a stable substitute.

## Properties

- **CAS:** 56-85-9
- **Working concentration:** 2 mM in complete medium
- **Half-life in medium:** ~1 week at 37 °C

## Common uses in this hypothesis space

- Standard supplement to basal media; cited in [[../sources/2024-diez-escudero-in-vitro-evaluation-of-pore-size-graded-bone]].

## Alternatives

- GlutaMAX (alanyl-glutamine) — stable, no ammonia accumulation.

## Gotchas / things to watch for

- Stock degrades fast — re-supplement long-running cultures or switch to GlutaMAX.
