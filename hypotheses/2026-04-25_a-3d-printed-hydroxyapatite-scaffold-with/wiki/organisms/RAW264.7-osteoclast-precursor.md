# RAW264.7 — mouse macrophage / osteoclast-precursor line

## What it is

RAW264.7 is a BALB/c mouse macrophage cell line transformed with Abelson murine leukemia virus, available from ATCC (TIB-71, CVCL_0493). Under RANKL stimulation (50–100 ng/mL, ± 25 ng/mL M-CSF) it differentiates to TRAP-positive multinucleated osteoclast-like cells in 4–7 days. It is the standard osteoclast-precursor model for bone-tissue-engineering studies that include resorption, osteoclast-osteoblast coupling, or scaffold-induced inflammation readouts ([[../sources/2025-qi-3d-printed-bioceramic-scaffolds-for-bone]], [[../sources/2015-chia-recent-advances-in-3d-printing-of-biomat]]).

## Growth conditions

- **Medium (proliferation)**: DMEM + 10% FBS + 1% P/S; 37 °C, 5% CO₂.
- **Medium (osteoclast induction)**: above + 50–100 ng/mL recombinant RANKL ± 25 ng/mL M-CSF; refresh every 2–3 days; readouts at days 4–7.
- **Subculturing**: scraping is preferred over trypsin (RAW264.7 detaches by gentle scraping; trypsin reduces viability).

## Why pick RAW264.7 for this hypothesis

- The hypothesis's primary endpoint is osteoblast proliferation, **not** osteoclast resorption. RAW264.7 is therefore peripheral here.
- However, an osteoclast-coupling arm with RAW264.7 + MC3T3-E1/hBMSC co-culture would address the [[../failure-modes/monoculture-misses-osteoclast-coupling]] critique — relevant if the hypothesis is extended toward in-vivo translation arguments.

## Common pitfalls

- [[../failure-modes/monoculture-misses-osteoclast-coupling]] — the central critique that an "osteoblast proliferation on scaffold" claim misses bone remodeling biology; RAW264.7 co-culture is the standard rebuttal.
- [[../failure-modes/cell-line-misidentification]] — RAW264.7 is well-authenticated; STR-authenticate on receipt nonetheless.
