# Mouse BMSC (primary)

## What it is

Primary mouse bone-marrow-derived MSCs, isolated by flushing the marrow cavity of long bones (femur, tibia) of an adult mouse and selecting plastic-adherent cells. The rodent analogue to hBMSC. Most commonly isolated from C57BL/6 mice. Used as a primary cell comparator alongside hBMSC for human-vs-mouse cross-species validation, or as a syngeneic cell source for mouse-defect-model implants.

## Growth conditions

- **Medium**: low-glucose DMEM or α-MEM + 10% FBS + 1% P/S; osteogenic medium adds dex + ascorbic-acid-2-phosphate + β-glycerophosphate.
- **Atmosphere**: 37 °C, 5% CO₂.
- **Passage cap**: ≤ P5–P6.

## Why pick mouse-BMSC-primary for this hypothesis

- Used in [[../sources/2024-wang-polydopamine-functionalized-calcium-defi]] alongside hBMSC for cross-species validation of polydopamine-functionalized CaP scaffolds.
- A syngeneic cell source for mouse cranial / femoral defect implant studies.
