# K7M2 — mouse osteosarcoma cell line

## What it is

K7M2 is a highly metastatic mouse osteosarcoma cell line derived in BALB/c, syngeneic to the BALB/c host. It is a tumor cell line, not an osteoblast model, and appears in this hypothesis only because [[../sources/2024-wang-polydopamine-functionalized-calcium-defi]] uses it in an anti-tumor / doxorubicin-loaded scaffold context (BALB/c mouse-osteosarcoma-model implant). Not relevant to gradient-pore osteoblast-proliferation experiments per se.

## Growth conditions

- **Medium**: DMEM + 10% FBS + 1% P/S; 37 °C, 5% CO₂.
