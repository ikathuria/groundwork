# Ultracentrifugation

## What it measures / produces

High-speed centrifugation (typically 100,000+ × g) separates particles by sedimentation rate (mass × density). Used in MV isolation, exosome isolation, and density-gradient subfractionation.

## When to use it

Companion technique to [[matrix-vesicle-isolation]]; otherwise not directly applicable to the present scaffold hypothesis.
