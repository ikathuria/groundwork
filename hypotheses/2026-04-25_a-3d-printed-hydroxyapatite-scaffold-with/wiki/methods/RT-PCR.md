# Reverse-transcription PCR (RT-PCR)

## What it measures / produces

RT-PCR converts mRNA to cDNA via reverse transcriptase, then amplifies via standard PCR for end-point detection (gel electrophoresis). Distinct from qPCR (real-time, quantitative); RT-PCR is end-point and qualitative or semi-quantitative.

## When to use it

Use RT-PCR for qualitative gene-expression confirmation when full qPCR is not needed. Most modern BTE workflows use qRT-PCR (real-time) instead.
