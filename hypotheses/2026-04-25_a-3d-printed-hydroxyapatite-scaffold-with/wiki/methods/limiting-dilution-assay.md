# Limiting dilution assay (LDA)

## What it measures / produces

Serial-dilution clonogenic assay used to estimate frequency of clonogenic / stem cells in a heterogeneous population. Cells are plated at multiple decreasing densities; positive wells (e.g., colonies, bone-forming grafts) are counted; Poisson statistics yield the limiting-cell-frequency.

## When to use it

Niche method for skeletal stem cell frequency quantification — used in [[../sources/protocol-2023-uchida]] paired with kidney capsule transplantation. Not directly applicable to the present scaffold hypothesis.
