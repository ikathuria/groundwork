# Peptide-amphiphile (PA) self-assembly

## What it measures / produces

Stupp-group peptide amphiphiles self-assemble into nanofibres that present nucleation epitopes for HA mineralisation. Output: biomimetic synthetic ECM templating ordered HA crystal growth. Reviewed in [[../sources/2008-palmer-biomimetic-systems-for-hydroxyapatite-mi]].

## When to use it

Niche method for biomimetic HA chemistry research; not directly applicable to gradient-pore scaffold fabrication for the present hypothesis.
