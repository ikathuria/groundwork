# Biphasic osteochondral bioreactor

## What it measures / produces

Dual-chamber perfusion bioreactor with two media circuits — chondrogenic on one side, osteogenic on the other — separated by the gradient scaffold. Output: scaffold with zonal differentiation matching the local medium and pore-size cue. Used in [[../sources/2025-conoscenti-continuous-pore-size-gradient-enhances-z]] for TIPS PLLA gradient scaffolds.

## When to use it

Niche for osteochondral interface scaffolds; not directly applicable to monolithic HA bone scaffolds.

## Step-level notes

- Chondrogenic medium on small-pore (70 μm) side; osteogenic medium on large-pore (200 μm) side.
- Even when both sides have identical medium, pore size alone biases differentiation [[../sources/2025-conoscenti-continuous-pore-size-gradient-enhances-z]].
