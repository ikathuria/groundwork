# Microfluidic gradient fabrication

## What it measures / produces

Microfluidic channels mix two solutions in controlled ratios to produce continuous composition gradients in hydrogel precursors, photopolymerised in place. Output: hydrogels with smooth biochemical / composition gradients.

## When to use it

Hydrogel-specific gradient method. Niche for HA scaffolds.
