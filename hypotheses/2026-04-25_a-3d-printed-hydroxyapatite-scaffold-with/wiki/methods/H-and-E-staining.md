# H&E (hematoxylin and eosin) staining

## What it measures / produces

Standard general histology stain: hematoxylin stains nuclei blue/purple; eosin stains cytoplasm and ECM pink. Output: tissue architecture, cellularity, gross histology. Used in [[../sources/2010-murphy-understanding-the-effect-of-mean-pore-size-on]] for cell-distribution histology in CG scaffolds.

## When to use it

General histology. Standard companion to specialised mineralisation stains (alizarin red, von Kossa).
