# Matrix vesicle isolation

## What it measures / produces

Sequential differential centrifugation + ultracentrifugation isolates matrix vesicles (MVs, ~100–200 nm extracellular vesicles) from mineralising osteoblast culture media. MVs are the primary HA-nucleation centres in osteoblast-mediated bone formation. Output: MV pellet for nanoparticle tracking analysis (NTA), SEM/EDX, and cell-free mineralisation assays.

## When to use it

Niche mechanistic method for studying scaffold-cultured osteoblast MV-mediated HA nucleation. Used in [[../sources/protocol-2025-sheikh-isolation-of-in-vitro-osteoblastic-deriv]] with MC3T3-E1 subclone 4.

## Things to watch for

- [[../failure-modes/mc3t3-e1-subclone-variability]] — subclone 4 was selected for mineralisation competence; other MC3T3-E1 subclones may not produce sufficient MVs.
