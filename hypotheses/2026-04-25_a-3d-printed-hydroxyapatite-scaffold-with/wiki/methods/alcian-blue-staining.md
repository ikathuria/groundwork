# Alcian blue staining

## What it measures / produces

Alcian blue stains sulfated and carboxylated glycosaminoglycans (GAGs) blue. Output: cartilage matrix proteoglycan / GAG-deposition staining; chondrogenic differentiation marker.

## When to use it

Use alcian blue for chondrogenic / cartilage matrix readouts. Used in [[../sources/2025-conoscenti-continuous-pore-size-gradient-enhances-z]] to mark chondrogenic deposition in the small-pore zone of TIPS PLLA gradient scaffolds (orthogonal to alizarin red for the osseous zone).
