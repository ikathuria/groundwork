# Mask-based photolithography gradient

## What it measures / produces

A photomask with spatially varying transmittance (grayscale) modulates UV dose across the sample, producing a crosslinking-density gradient. Output: hydrogel or photoresin with smooth stiffness gradient.

## When to use it

Hydrogel-specific gradient method. Niche for HA scaffolds.
