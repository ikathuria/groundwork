# Western blot

## What it measures / produces

SDS-PAGE separation of proteins followed by transfer to membrane and probing with primary antibody + chemiluminescent / fluorescent secondary antibody. Output: protein-level expression, semi-quantitative band intensity.

## When to use it

Use Western blot to confirm protein-level expression of osteogenic markers (RUNX2, OCN, COL1, OPN) in addition to mRNA-level qPCR. Used by [[../sources/2024-wang-polydopamine-functionalized-calcium-defi]] for ALP / RUNX2 / OPN protein quantification on gradient CDHA scaffolds.

## Things to watch for

- [[../failure-modes/donor-variability-hMSC]] — primary hMSC variability between donors.
